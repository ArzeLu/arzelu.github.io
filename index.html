Hello! Welcome to my first ever web page that I'm so excited to show you!
I am Arze and I'm a student pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Science. I graduated with the Bachelor's degree just a few months prior.
The interest in the major comes from my strong passion in crafting. As a kid, I would save any cardboard box in case any idea sprung into mind. I made a L-shaped desk out of cardboard, a sword style pencil case, and a lot of toys any child would dream to own. Growing up, I always struggled with acquiring the right material to build higher quality craft, just like the carousel I just finished that required rubber wheels and a few electrical components. For that reason, I chose Computer Science over EE and Mechanical Engineering. I was ecstatic about the thought of building computer projects nearly without a cost. After achieving the Bachelor's, I started building this website, an app, and a vpn, although only the website is at a near finished state.

I have some hobbies aside from programming. I can't resist myself from playing chess whenever I have free time. It is probably the best puzzle game out there. I also draw from time to time, and I try to be consistent daily with my workout routine. If it weren't for newly grow-up responsibilities, I would spend all my time on the countless relatively quiescent activities.

Nice to meet all of you that visited my page!

On the last note, I'd like to give thanks to my family, my close friends, and my beautiful and caring girlfriend for making everything possible.
